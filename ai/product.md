# Product brief

## What BoardApp is
BoardApp is a **Swedish-first board portal** for:
- owner-led companies
- PE-backed portfolio companies

It helps boards run governance work end-to-end in one place, with legal/process support aligned to Swedish operating norms.

## Core user outcomes
- Run board work faster with less admin overhead.
- Keep ownership and governance records accurate and audit-ready.
- Turn decisions into accountable follow-up actions.
- Improve board quality through structured meeting cadence and documentation.

## Core modules (v1 scope)
1. **Cap table**: Ownership overview, dilution visibility, share classes, exportable snapshots.
2. **Aktiebok (share register)**: Statutory register integrity, transaction history, role-based access.
3. **Meeting lifecycle**: Agenda prep, board packs, invitations, minutes, sign-off.
4. **Decisions**: Formal decision log linked to meetings and owners.
5. **Follow-ups**: Action items with owner, due date, and status tracking.

## Target personas
- Chair / board lead
- CEO / founder
- Board secretary / legal operations
- Investor representative (PE operating partner, board member)

## Product principles
- Swedish-first language, terminology, and templates.
- Compliance-by-default without creating legal lock-in.
